Tracking and Opting-out

brandonz.me tracks visitor information to understand how people are finding this site, how well it's meeting their needs, and if there are any code bugs that need squashing.

If you'd rather not have information related to your visit tracked, here's how you can opt-out:

Google Analytics Use their opt-out browser extension.

Heap Analytics Use the Ghostery browser extension.

Hotjar Insights Turn on "Do Not Track" in your browser.
