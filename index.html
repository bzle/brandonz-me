Infolinx

Website Redesign

Infolinx is a leading company in the records management software industry. I worked as their in-house designer for five years and had the oppurtunity to tackle various web and print projects.

The goal of this redesign was to make the content more digestible and give Infolinx greater first impression credibility. The content was improved by breaking up the main text and adding photos and icons as compliments. To increase perceived credibility, a full-width design with large images was used to help convey that Infolinx is a company who’s staying current in the tech industry.
2

Trible

Web App / Website Design

Trible is a side-project my business partner, Matt Steele, and I have been working on. The idea is to create a web app where people can read the Bible in a more social way than current online Bibles.

The bread and butter of this social interaction is being able to read, post, and reply to questions and comments on any verse in the Bible. The goal of this social interaction is to lift one another up, that we might be encouraged and better understand the Bible.
3

Bravo Landscaping

Website Redesign

This website redesign is for a landscaping company in Texas. As evidenced by the pictures of their work, they run a high quality operation. Their previous site didn’t do their work credit and might have been a barrier for attracting some clients.

This redesign puts the photos of their work on display in a bold way, allowing them to speak for themselves. Additionally, the clean custom design should inspire trust and confidence for first time visitors to the site.
4

Freestate Gymnastics

Website Redesign

Growing up, I had the privilege of learning gymnastics and trampoline at Freestate and eventually instructing children as a teacher.

The goal of this redesign is to show off, visually, the many opportunities Freestate provides for people looking for an athletic outlet. In addition to that, the design and photos are meant to convey that Freestate is a place where you can learn to excel while having fun.
